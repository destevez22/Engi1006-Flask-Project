So far my two favorite classes have been Mechanics and Python. As an inspiring mechanical engineering major it is certainly a good thing that I like mechanics, even if sometimes I irresponsibly take way too much time to solve a frustrating problem. My relationship with python began a bit more begrudginly; I took the class as a requirement but also out of interest, somewhat intimidated by my lack of coding knowledge. Throughout the semester there were frustrating moments, and far too often a lack of knowledge discouraged me, but I certainly have grown to like python, both as a coding language and a class.
